Mar Dionysius College, Pazhanji, is an esteemed institution affiliated with the University of Calicut, dedicated to academic excellence and holistic development since 1982.

The college offers a diverse range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs across multiple disciplines, fostering innovation, research, and critical thinking among students.

With state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and a commitment to social responsibility, Mar Dionysius College continues to shape future leaders and scholars.

  • • Maximum 500 words
  • • Include title, keywords, and author details
  • • Author details: name, designation, institution, email, contact number

Full Paper Guidelines

  • • 6000-7000 words (approx. 8-12 pages)
  • • MS Word format, Times New Roman, Font 12, 1.5 spacing
  • • APA/MLA 9th edition citation format
  • • Double-blind peer review process
